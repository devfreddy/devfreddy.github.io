  • What to Bring: Comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, swimwear, light layers for evenings

  • Hiking: Athletic or hiking shoes required for Caldera trail
  • Sites: Some archaeological sites have uneven surfaces and many steps
  • Dietary Requirements: Please notify guides/restaurants in advance
